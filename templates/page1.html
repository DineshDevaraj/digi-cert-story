Digital Certificate Story

  • This story will give you a shallow explanation of end-to-end working of digital-certificate without getting into the depths of technical details.
  • This will be a (RPG) role play game like story line. You will take role of different entities involved in working of digital certificate like Certificate Authority, Web Browser and Organisation that needs the certificate.

Disclaimer

  • This story telling doesn't talk on the basics of the digital-certificate. Instead it tries to explain the end-to-end flow of how the digital-certificate works.
  • Hence the target reader of this story is expected to know the basic terminologies of digital-certifate like, public-private key pair, certificate authority etc...
  • Also to have explanation less complicated this story has slight technical approximations. You can refer to the actual standard for accurate details.
