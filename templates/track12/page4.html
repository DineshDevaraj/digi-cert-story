{{ page }}. MicroAuth's (CSR) Certifcate Sign Request.

  1. Now MicroAuth has both priv & pub key. Next it should generate a CSR & self-signed certificate.
  2. Generate (CSR) Certificate Sign Request. openssl req -new -key micrcauth.priv -out micrcauth.csr -subj "/C=US/ST=California/L=San Francisco/O=Microcert CA Inc./CN=micrcauth.com"
  3. View micrcauth.csr generated in previous step. openssl req -in micrcauth.csr -text -noout -verify
  4. Now there is a CSR next go ahead and create a self-signed certificate.
