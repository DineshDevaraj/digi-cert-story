{{ page }}. Apply for Digital Certificate

  1. Rothchild Bank needs to submit (CSR) Certificate Signing Request to MicroAuth.
  2. Details to be submitted for certificate request are bank name, address, email Id, public-key, signature. These details are called (CRI) Certificate Request Informaion. Please refer to CSR & CRI standard for more detailed and accurate information.
  3. Did you see what are the last two fields in previous point. That's right they are the public-key and signature.
  4. First of all Rothichild Bank needs to generate its own public-private key pair, generate the signature of CSR using the private-key and add the signature back into the same CSR file.
  5. Go ahead and submit the (.csr file) certificate request to MicroAuth at support@micrcauth.com
